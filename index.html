Our Story

Rooted in Purpose,
Growing with Vision

Asomdwee Ntonton Som-School was founded with a singular conviction: that every child deserves access to quality education that respects their identity and prepares them for the future. Our name — meaning "School of Peaceful Progress" — reflects our philosophy of purposeful, harmonious growth.

Over the years, we have grown from a small community school into a respected institution known for academic excellence, character development, and a nurturing environment that honours both tradition and innovation.

We are more than a school. We are a family of learners, teachers, and dreamers committed to the belief that education has the power to transform lives and entire communities.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

Foundation

The School is Established

Asomdwee Ntonton Som-School opens its doors with a founding vision: to provide exceptional education grounded in cultural values and academic rigour to the local community.

Early Growth

Expanding Our Programmes

Introduction of science, arts, and vocational tracks, alongside structured extracurricular programmes in sports, music, and leadership — marking the school's evolution into a truly holistic institution.

Recognition

National Accreditation Achieved

The school receives full national accreditation and begins producing consistently high examination results, earning recognition among the top-performing schools in the region.

Today

A Growing Legacy

With over 1,200 enrolled students, a dedicated faculty, and a thriving alumni network, Asomdwee Ntonton Som-School continues to set the standard for education that shapes whole human beings.
